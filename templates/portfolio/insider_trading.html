💡 Why track insider trading?
Corporate insiders — executives, directors, and major shareholders — must report their trades to the SEC. When insiders buy their own stock, it's often seen as a bullish signal since they know the company best. Insider selling can have many reasons (diversification, taxes) and is less meaningful on its own. Data sourced from public SEC filings via Yahoo Finance.
