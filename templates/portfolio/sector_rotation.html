💡 What is Sector Rotation?
Sector rotation is the movement of investment capital from one industry sector to another. During different economic cycles, certain sectors outperform others. Technology tends to lead in bull markets, while Utilities and Consumer Staples are defensive in downturns. Tracking sector momentum helps identify where the smart money is moving.
